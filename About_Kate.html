Kate Allstadt

 
 

I’m interested in unusual and multidisciplinary applications of seismology in the exciting and very geologically active Pacific Northwest. Most of my research involves at least one of the following: landslides, volcanoes, glaciers, and of course earthquakes


Interests Seismology, engineering seismology, hazard and disaster mitigation, landslides, seismically induced landslides, multidisciplinary applications of seismology, volcano seismology.


Education

  2. *M.S. Engineering Seismology (2009), dual degree, Universite Joseph Fourier, Grenoble, France and ROSE School, Pavia, Italy

  3. *B.S. Environmental Geology (2008), Northeastern University, Boston
